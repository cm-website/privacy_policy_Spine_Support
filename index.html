Privacy Policy

Effective as of: 2026-02-06

This privacy policy applies to the Curva - Spine & Support app ("Application") for mobile devices that was created by Elaina ("Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages
  • The time spent on the Application
  • The operating system you use on your mobile device

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Account creation timestamp

Pressure Sensor Data

When you use the app with your scoliosis brace, we collect:

  • Real-time pressure readings from sensors via Bluetooth
  • Session timestamps and duration
  • Pressure alerts and notifications

This data is essential for monitoring your brace usage and providing insights about your treatment compliance.

Device Connection

We collect Bluetooth connection data to pair with your brace monitoring device. This includes device identifiers necessary for establishing and maintaining the connection.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide real-time pressure monitoring
  • Track your brace wear time and compliance
  • Generate daily and historical reports
  • Send alerts when pressure thresholds are exceeded
  • Power the AI assistant feature
  • Analyze user behavior and improve app performance

Third-Party Access

The Service Provider may share collected information with third-party services as described below. Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services to aid in improving the Application.

Firebase (Google)

We use Firebase Realtime Database to securely store your account information and session data. Firebase is operated by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Your data is stored securely and is only accessible to you when logged into your account.

OpenAI

Our AI chat assistant feature uses OpenAI's API to provide personalized guidance about scoliosis brace usage. When you use the AI assistant:

  • Your chat messages are sent to OpenAI for processing
  • Summarized pressure data may be shared to provide context
  • OpenAI processes this data according to their Privacy Policy
  • We do not share your personal account information with OpenAI

The AI assistant is designed to provide general guidance and is not a substitute for medical advice.

Legal Disclosure

The Service Provider may disclose collected information:

  • As required by law, such as compliance with legal processes
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users or investigate fraud
  • To trusted service providers who work on behalf of the Service Provider

Data Storage and Security

The Service Provider takes precautions to protect user data through physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards. Your data is stored securely in Firebase Realtime Database with the following protections:

  • Authentication required for all data access
  • Data is encrypted in transit
  • Access is limited to your own user account

Data Retention Policy

The Service Provider will retain User Provided data for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter. You can delete your account at any time through the app settings, which will permanently remove:

  • Your user profile
  • All pressure session data
  • All AI summary data

If you'd like the Service Provider to delete your data, please contact hello@codingmind.com.

Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all collection of information by the Application by uninstalling it. You may also opt out of using the AI assistant feature at any time.

Children

The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. This app may be used by minors under parental supervision for scoliosis treatment monitoring. Parents and guardians are encouraged to monitor their children's Internet usage and enforce this policy.

If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact hello@codingmind.com for necessary action.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Users are advised to check this page for updates. Any changes will be posted in the app.

Your Consent

By using the Application, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

Contact Us
